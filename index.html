Get started with WareIQ Shipping

Follow this step-by-step guide to set up your account, configure shipping rates, and process your first order.

Step 1 of 9
Create Your Account
Sign up at webapp.wareiq.com/signup with your business details.
Cut: 0:00 – 1:30
Account Sign Up
Video snippet: 0:00 – 1:30
1
Go to webapp.wareiq.com/signup
Open the WareIQ Shipping web app in your browser. You will see the Sign Up page with the WareIQ Shipping logo on the left and the registration form on the right.
2
Sign up with Google or fill in the form
You can click "Sign up with Google" for faster registration, or fill in the form manually with your First Name, Last Name, Phone Number, Email Address, and Company Name.
All fields are required
3
Click the Sign Up button
Once all fields are filled in, the "Sign Up" button turns blue and becomes active. Click it to create your account. You will receive a verification email.
4
Log in with your credentials
After verification, go to webapp.wareiq.com/login. Enter your email address and password, then click "Login". You can also log in via Google.
Tip: Use "Login with Google" for the fastest access
⚠️
Already have an account? Click "Already have an account? Login" on the signup page, or go directly to webapp.wareiq.com/login.
📋
Step 2 of 9
KYC Details
Complete your KYC to verify your business identity and activate shipping.
Cut: 1:30 – 3:00
KYC Details Setup
Video snippet: 1:30 – 3:00
1
The onboarding wizard opens automatically
After login, you are taken to the dashboard and the 5-step onboarding wizard starts. Step 1 is KYC Details. The left panel shows your progress: KYC Details, Terms & Conditions, Wallet Recharge, Configure Shipping Rate, and Integrate your channel.
2
Fill in your business details
Complete all required fields in the KYC form.
3
Verify your mobile number via OTP
Enter your Mobile Number and click "Send OTP". A 6-digit OTP will be sent to your number. Enter it in the OTP Verification popup, then click "Verify". The number will show a green checkmark once verified.
OTP verification is mandatory
4
Upload your PAN Card and GST Certificate
Enter your PAN number and upload a copy of your PAN card. If you have GST, check the "GST Available" box, enter your GST number, and upload the GST Certificate. You can drag and drop files or click "Add file".
GST is optional but recommended for B2B shipments
FieldNotes
Seller Name*Your brand or trading name
Legal Name*Name as per registration documents
Mobile No.*10-digit number — must be OTP verified
Company Address*Registered business address
City, State, Pincode*Must match your address documents
PAN No.*10-character PAN (e.g. ABCDE1234F)
GST No.Optional — required only if GST registered

Step 3 of 9
Terms & Conditions
Review and sign the WareIQ service agreement to activate your account.
Cut: 3:00 – 3:30
Terms and Conditions
Video snippet: 3:00 – 3:30
1
Read the Service Agreement
The Terms & Conditions page shows WareIQ's full Service Agreement covering services, charges/billing, and SLAs. You can also download it using the download icon at the top right of the agreement.
2
Enter your Name and Designation
At the bottom of the page, fill in your Name and Designation fields. These serve as your electronic signature on the agreement.
Both fields are required to proceed
3
Click "Save & Continue"
Click the blue "Save & Continue" button at the bottom right. This completes Step 2 of the onboarding wizard and marks it with a green checkmark.
ℹ️
Billing note: Monthly invoices are raised by the 5th of each month for the previous month. Shipping charges are deducted from your prepaid wallet simultaneously when orders are shipped.
💳
Step 4 of 9
Wallet Recharge
Add funds to your WareIQ wallet to start shipping orders.
Cut: 3:30 – 4:00
Wallet Recharge
Video snippet: 3:30 – 4:00
1
Go to Wallet Recharge (Step 3 in wizard)
After accepting T&C, the wizard moves to Wallet Recharge. Your current balance is always visible in the top navigation bar. WareIQ Shipping works on a prepaid wallet model.
2
Click "Recharge" to add funds
Click the "Recharge" button in the top navigation (or in the onboarding step). Choose your recharge amount and complete the payment via your preferred method.
You must have a non-zero balance to process orders
⚠️
Low balance warning: A red banner will appear at the top of your dashboard when your wallet balance is low. Recharge promptly to avoid order processing delays.
📦
Step 5 of 9
Configure Shipping Rates
Select your shipping rate plan. WareIQ offers Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers.
Cut: 3:30 – 4:00
Shipping Rate Configuration
Video snippet: 3:30 – 4:00
1
View the rate card
The rate card shows all courier partners across rate tiers: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum. Each tier shows base weight, additional weight offset, and rates by zone: Within City, Within State, Metro to Metro, Rest of India, and North East & J&K.
2
Review courier partners and rates
Available couriers include Ecom Express, Amazon Shipping, DTDC, Delhivery, WIQ Bluedart Surface, and more. Each courier also shows COD charges and COD percentage.
3
Activate the plan via App Store
Go to App Store in the left navigation, find "Shipping Rates", and click Activate. A green "Application activated successfully" toast will confirm activation.
Rate plan must be activated before creating orders

Step 6 of 9
Add Pickup / Return Address
Set up your warehouse or pickup location where couriers will collect shipments.
Cut: 4:30 – 5:00
Add Pickup Address
Video snippet: 4:30 – 5:00
1
Open the Add Pickup/Return form
Navigate to Settings and open the Pickup/Return address form. This is accessible during onboarding or any time from Settings.
2

Step 7 of 9
Integrate Your Sales Channel
Connect WareIQ Shipping to your storefront so orders sync automatically.
Cut: 5:00 – 7:00
Channel Integration
Video snippet: 5:00 – 7:00
1
Go to Step 5 in the onboarding wizard: Integrate your channel
The integration screen shows all available sales channels. Choose the one that matches your storefront.
2
Click "Integrate" next to your channel
For Shopify: click the Integrate button, enter your Store URL (e.g. yourstore.myshopify.com — without https://), select Integration Type as Sales Channel, and click Submit. This redirects you to Shopify to install the WareIQ app.
Do not include https:// in the Shopify Store URL
3
Approve the Shopify app installation
Shopify will ask you to approve the WareIQ app installation. Click Accept. You will be redirected back to the WareIQ webapp once connected.
4
Or upload manually if skipping integration for now
If you want to skip channel integration for now, click "Upload Manually". You can always come back and connect a channel later from Settings > Channels.
You can add multiple channels after setup

Step 8 of 9
Courier Allocation Settings
Configure how WareIQ automatically assigns courier partners to your orders.
Cut: 7:00 – 8:00
Courier Settings
Video snippet: 7:00 – 8:00
1
Go to Settings > Couriers
Navigate to Settings in the left sidebar, then click Couriers. You will see four tabs: Courier Allocation, All Couriers, Activated Couriers, and Deactivated Couriers.
2
Enable Auto Shipping (recommended)
The "Auto Shipping" toggle is ON by default. This lets WareIQ automatically select the best courier for each order based on serviceability and cost. Leave it enabled unless you want manual courier selection.
Recommended for most brands
3
Create Custom Rules (optional)
If you want specific routing logic, click "Create Custom Rule". You can create rules based on: Delivery Zone, Pickup Point, Weight, SKU, Delivery Pincode, Payment Mode, Order Amount, and Channel.
Advanced — set up after your first few orders if needed
ℹ️
Custom rules example: You can create a rule that always uses DTDC for prepaid orders above Rs. 2000 going to metro cities, while using Delhivery for all COD orders.
🏢
Step 9 of 9
Complete Your Company Profile
Add your brand details, logo, and account settings for invoices and tracking pages.
Cut: 8:00 – 8:33
Company Profile
Video snippet: 8:00 – 8:33
1
Go to Settings > Company Profile
Navigate to Settings in the left sidebar, then click Company Profile. You will see three tabs: Contact Details, Account Details, and KYC Details.
2
Fill in Contact Details
Enter your Communication Name, Company Address, and upload your Brand Logo. The platform tooltip will guide you through each field when you first visit.
3
Review Account Details and KYC tabs
Check the Account Details tab for your Client Code and Primary Email. Verify the KYC Details tab shows your PAN, GST, and address as submitted. Edit any details if needed.
4
Save your profile
Click Save at the bottom right. Your company name and logo will now appear on branded tracking pages and invoices.
